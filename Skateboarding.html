Craziest Skateboard Tricks

Casper Flip

A half kickflip caught almost upside down with the front foot under the front truck and the back foot on the tail, then flipped back over with a shuvit heelflip like motion.

Try a hospital flip instead. It looks the same but you only need to use your front foot.
